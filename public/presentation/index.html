90–120s script

Every month, millions of Pakistani families open their electricity bill hoping it won’t be too high. The problem is not just expensive electricity—it’s that people don’t know they’re overspending until the bill arrives. By then, it’s too late.

That’s why we built ZAPP. ZAPP is an electricity planning assistant. Instead of simply tracking usage, it combines your appliances, monthly unit target, weather forecast, and solar availability to create a personalized daily plan that tells you the best time to use electricity.

The result is simple: less guesswork, smarter energy decisions, better use of solar, and fewer surprises at the end of the month.

Our vision is to make electricity planning as intelligent and accessible as navigation apps made travel—helping every household in Pakistan know tomorrow’s electricity costs before tomorrow arrives.
